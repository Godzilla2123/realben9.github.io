Keys

How to get them

Firstly, to get here you did 1 of 3 things.

A : You found part of a key. | B : You viewed the repository. | C : You manually typed this URL in.

If you did A, you know what they look like. Otherwise, this is how you get them :

At the bottom-right corner of the screen, there may be part of a key. (you may need to explore the website first.)

Instead of the word EXAMPLE, they will have a number, followed by 3 bytes.

After you have found part of a key, you can either remember it (although there are several parts of a key), or write it down.

What they do

Firstly, let's assume there are 8 parts.

[EXAMPLE]

1 - 8fb3d4

2 - 78a2c1

3 - 64e90d

4 - da4c3f

5 - 1a2b3c

6 - deefab

7 - 947382

8 - 42f69c

Next, there are 3 steps.

Step 1 : Go to the main page.

Step 2 : Add all 8 parts to the URL.

Step 3 : Add .html to the new URL.

EXAMPLE : https://realben9.github.io/8fb3d478a2c164e90dda4c3f1a2b3cdeefab94738242f69c.html

If you have found all of them, (and typed it correctly,) this should lead to a secret page.

EXAMPLE - 69f420
